Got Questions?

Everything you need to know.

Browse our collection online, choose your gown, and message us on WhatsApp to schedule a fitting appointment at our KL boutique. We hold the gown for you, fit it, make any alterations, and you collect it before your event. Return it after — we handle dry-cleaning.

We offer 3-day, 5-day, and 7-day rental periods. The standard rental is 3 days, which is ideal for most events. Extended rentals are perfect for destination weddings or multi-day shoots. Pricing varies by duration — ask us on WhatsApp for a quote.

Yes — free in-store alterations are included with every rental. Our resident seamstress will ensure the gown fits your body perfectly during your fitting appointment. Minor adjustments such as hemming, side-taking, and strap adjustments are all covered.

Always. Every gown is professionally dry-cleaned between every rental. You never need to worry about returning a clean gown — just return it as it was worn. We handle everything.

Yes, all fittings and collections are by appointment only. This ensures our full attention is on you. Walk-ins may be accommodated subject to availability, but we strongly recommend booking in advance — especially during prom and wedding seasons.

A refundable security deposit is required at the time of collection. The exact amount varies by gown. The deposit is returned in full upon the gown's safe return, provided there is no damage beyond normal wear.

We are located at 3rd Mile Square, Jalan Klang Lama, 58000 Kuala Lumpur. We are open Monday to Saturday, 11am–8pm, and Sunday 12pm–6pm. All visits are by appointment — message us on WhatsApp to book your slot.

Absolutely. We recommend reserving your gown at least 2–3 weeks before your event, especially for peak seasons like prom (May–July) and wedding season. Message us the gown name and your event date to check availability and secure your booking.
